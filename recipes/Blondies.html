Blondie Cookies

Crispy and delicious chocolate chip cubes

Ingredients:

  • 150g butter
  • 270ml brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 350ml flour
  • teaspoon vanilla extract
  • teaspoon drinking soda
  • chopped walnuts
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. mix the butter wite the sugar
  2. add the rest of the Ingredients except the chocolate chips and nix it up
  3. flatten in the baking tray and sprinkle the chocolate chips
  4. bake on 180 degrees until the dough dries
